Research Interests<br><br>
======
* Human-computer interaction (HCI), especially interfaces incorporating haptic and auditory feedback.

* Movement as an interaction design element, including gestural input and motion display.

* Embodied Cognition and its application within HCI.
<br><br>

Education<br><br>
======
* Ph.D in Computer-based Music Theory, Stanford University, Stanford, CA, 1994-2000, Dissertation: “Playing by Feel: Incorporating Haptic Feedback into Computer-based Musical Instruments”

* Postgraduate Certificate in Higher Education and Training, Queen’s University Belfast, 2008

* M.S. in Music Technology, University of York, York, England, 1988-1989, Project: “Computerized Processing of 3-Dimensional Sound”

* Licentiate. Piano Teaching Diploma, Trinity College London, England, 1989

* B.A. in Music, with honors, Trinity College Dublin, Ireland, 1984-1988
